Dhaka Boat Club is a community club, located on the bank of river Turag. Every human being has a innate quality of curiosity and the urge to follow something, the urge to know more about what pleases him, this develops into an interest, and then into a hobby such as boating – a desire, which even transcends the realm of leisure time indulgence, a passion that led to the inception of the Dhaka Boat Club.

How To Become A Member

To become a member of the club, a person needs to be refereed by an existing member of the club. There are also fees depending on the membership type.

Library

Dhaka Boat Club features a small library at the old Club building. Despite its humble size, the library is well stocked with a diverse collection of books, novels, magazines, newspapers and journals. The library is equipped to aid Members with research material, as well as keeping one engaged with a variety of content for casual reading
